# Glossary

## Key Terms

### Portal

**Definition:** A secure space for your client to upload and see requests.

**Usage:**
- Business feature only
- Each client gets their own portal
- Clients access via secure token login
- Uploads land in your organization

**Example:**
> "Create a portal for your client, then send document requests. The client uploads directly into their portal, and you see everything organized in your Documents."

### Share

**Definition:** A PIN-protected link to view/download a document with watermark.

**Usage:**
- Available in both Business and Personal
- Optional PIN and expiry
- Watermark viewer
- Can be revoked anytime

**Example:**
> "Create a share link with a PIN and 7-day expiry. The recipient sees a watermarked viewer and can download if you allow it."

### Labels

**Definition:** Quick tags like ID, Bills, Receipts used for search and automation.

**Usage:**
- Apply to documents
- Drive search results
- Enable automation
- Organize content

**Example:**
> "Apply labels like 'Tax Document' or 'Receipt' to documents. Then search by label to find everything instantly."

### Caps

**Definition:** Monthly usage limits (shown as bars; internal cost math is hidden).

**Usage:**
- Displayed in Billing page
- Alert at 70/80/90/95%
- Hard stop at 100%
- Grace to 103%

**Example:**
> "Your plan has usage caps for storage, OCR pages, and share links. You'll see alerts as you approach your limits."

### OCR

**Definition:** Optical Character Recognition - automatic text extraction from documents.

**Usage:**
- Processes PDFs and images
- Extracts searchable text
- Enables full-text search
- Counts against OCR pages limit

**Example:**
> "OCR automatically extracts text from your documents, making them searchable. Upload a receipt, and you can search for the merchant name or amount."

### Client Portal

**Definition:** A dedicated secure space for each client to upload documents and view requests.

**Usage:**
- Business feature
- One portal per client
- Secure token access
- Request-based workflow

**Example:**
> "Create a client portal, invite your client, then send document requests. The client uploads directly into their portal."

### Request

**Definition:** A document request sent to a client portal with a template and due date.

**Usage:**
- Business feature
- Uses templates
- Sent to portals
- Tracks completion

**Example:**
> "Send a KYC document request to your client's portal. They upload the documents, and you see everything organized."

### Template

**Definition:** A ready-made vault structure (folders, labels, actions) from the Marketplace.

**Usage:**
- Install from Marketplace
- Preview before installing
- Dry run available
- Customizable after install

**Example:**
> "Install the 'Personal Life Admin' template to get folders for IDs, Bills, Receipts, and Tax documents with month-by-month organization."

### Watermark

**Definition:** A visible overlay on shared documents showing organization/user information.

**Usage:**
- Applied to all shares
- Prevents unauthorized sharing
- Cannot be easily removed
- Includes user/org info

**Example:**
> "When you share a document, recipients see it in a watermarked viewer. The watermark includes your organization name and helps protect your documents."

### PWA

**Definition:** Progressive Web App - installable web app that works offline.

**Usage:**
- Install on any device
- Works offline
- Syncs when online
- App-like experience

**Example:**
> "Install SecureVault Docs as a PWA on your phone or tablet. It works offline and syncs when you're back online."

### Email-to-Vault

**Definition:** A unique email address that automatically adds forwarded documents to your vault.

**Usage:**
- Personal and Business
- Forward receipts/bills
- Auto-labeling
- Automatic organization

**Example:**
> "Forward receipts to your Email-to-Vault address. They automatically land in your vault with labels applied."

### Dry Run

**Definition:** Simulating a template installation without making actual changes.

**Usage:**
- Preview template structure
- Check for conflicts
- Test before installing
- No changes made

**Example:**
> "Use Dry Run to preview what a template will install. You can see all the folders and labels without making any changes."

### Audit Log

**Definition:** A record of all actions taken in the system (uploads, shares, settings changes).

**Usage:**
- Admins can view
- Support can access
- Filter by user/action
- Security tracking

**Example:**
> "The audit log shows all activity in your organization - who uploaded what, when shares were created, and any settings changes."

### Connector

**Definition:** An integration with external services (QBO, Google Drive, etc.).

**Usage:**
- Connect external accounts
- Sync data (mock in test)
- Import files
- Automate workflows

**Example:**
> "Connect your Google Drive to import files directly into SecureVault Docs. Files sync automatically."

### Usage Bar

**Definition:** A visual indicator showing current usage against plan limits.

**Usage:**
- Displayed in Billing
- Color-coded by threshold
- Shows percentage
- Alerts at thresholds

**Example:**
> "The usage bar shows you're at 75% of your storage limit. You'll get an alert at 80%."

### Marketplace

**Definition:** A collection of ready-made templates for vault organization.

**Usage:**
- Browse templates
- Preview before installing
- Install to Business or Personal
- Manage installed templates

**Example:**
> "Browse the Marketplace to find templates for accounting, personal organization, or business workflows. Install with one click."
