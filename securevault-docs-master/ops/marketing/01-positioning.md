# Positioning & Narrative

## One-Liner (Homepage Hero)

**Capture once. Organize forever.** Secure, simple document workflows for teams and individuals.

## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)

SecureVault Docs turns messy files and emails into searchable, shareable, audit-ready records. Upload from anywhere, invite clients, request documents, and stay within budget using automatic usage caps and alerts. Works great for personal finances and scales for teams.

## Value Pillars

### Business

1. **Client Portals that clients actually use** – one link, PIN/expiry, drag-and-drop uploads.

2. **Fast intake → clean vault** – OCR + smart labels; find anything in seconds.

3. **Guardrails, not surprises** – transparent usage meters and alerts.

4. **Share without risk** – watermarks, PINs, expiries, and access logs.

### Personal

1. **Everything important, in one place** – IDs, taxes, receipts, warranties.

2. **Smart organization** – auto labels, reminders, monthly folders.

3. **Easy hand-off** – share a time-boxed, PIN-protected link when needed.

## Why Now

Email attachments, scattered drives, and compliance pressure make simple workflows invaluable. Modern teams want cost control and an experience clients understand on the first try.

## Competitive Differentiation

- **vs. Email**: Organized portals vs. scattered threads
- **vs. Drive folders**: Client-friendly uploads vs. permission confusion
- **vs. Generic storage**: Purpose-built for document workflows vs. general file storage
- **vs. Enterprise tools**: Simple pricing vs. complex enterprise contracts

## Target Audience

**Primary**: Small-mid firms (5-100 seats) managing recurring client paperwork
**Secondary**: Busy professionals & families consolidating personal documents

## Key Messages

- **Simplicity**: One link, no confusion
- **Security**: PINs, watermarks, expiry dates
- **Transparency**: Usage meters, clear pricing
- **Speed**: OCR, smart labels, instant search
