## Objection Handling

### "Email is fine."

**Response:**
> "Email works, but portals cut back-and-forth. Clients upload in one place with reminders, and you get organized packets automatically. No more searching through email threads."

**Follow-up:**
- Show demo of portal upload
- Compare email vs. portal workflow
- Highlight time savings

### "Hidden costs?"

**Response:**
> "No hidden costs. We show usage meters with alerts at 70, 80, 90, and 95 percent. Hard stop at 103 percent. You always know where you are, and you can upgrade anytime."

**Follow-up:**
- Show usage meters in demo
- Explain alert system
- Compare to competitors

### "Security?"

**Response:**
> "Security is built in. PIN-protected links, expiry dates, watermark viewers, and audit logs. SSO is on our roadmap for enterprise customers."

**Follow-up:**
- Show security features
- Explain compliance capabilities
- Share security documentation

### "We already have [competitor]."

**Response:**
> "That's great. What's working well, and what could be better? Many teams switch to us for simpler pricing, better client experience, and transparent usage meters."

**Follow-up:**
- Understand their pain points
- Show differentiation
- Offer migration support

### "Too expensive."

**Response:**
> "Let's look at your current costs. Many teams save time and reduce errors, which often pays for itself. Plus, you can start with Starter and upgrade as you grow."

**Follow-up:**
- Calculate ROI
- Show cost comparison
- Offer trial extension

## Demo Flow (15 Minutes)

### 1. Invite → Client Portal Upload (3 min)

**What to Show:**
- Create client portal
- Send invite
- Show client upload interface
- Demonstrate drag-and-drop

**What to Say:**
> "Create a portal for your client with one click. They get a secure link, upload files directly, and everything lands organized in your vault."

### 2. Share Link with Watermark & Expiry (3 min)

**What to Show:**
- Create share link
- Set PIN and expiry
- Show watermark viewer
- Demonstrate access tracking

**What to Say:**
> "Share documents securely with PIN protection and expiry dates. Recipients see a watermarked viewer, and you can track access and revoke anytime."

### 3. OCR Label + Search (3 min)

**What to Show:**
- Upload document
- Show OCR processing
- Apply labels
- Search by label or content

**What to Say:**
> "OCR extracts text automatically, and you can apply labels for instant search. Find any document by name, date, or content in seconds."

### 4. Usage Meters Demo (3 min)

**What to Show:**
- Billing page
- Usage bars
- Alert thresholds
- Upgrade path

**What to Say:**
> "See exactly where you are with transparent usage meters. Alerts warn you at 70, 80, 90, and 95 percent. No surprises."

### 5. Pricing Selection → Checkout (3 min)

**What to Show:**
- Pricing page
- Plan comparison
- Annual vs. monthly
- Checkout flow

**What to Say:**
> "Choose the plan that fits your needs. Start with Starter and upgrade as you grow. Save 10% with annual billing."
