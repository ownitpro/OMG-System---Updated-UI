## Risk Mitigation

### Design Partner Recruitment
- **Risk:** Low interest
- **Mitigation:** Offer extended free trial, priority support, feature input

### Case Study Collection
- **Risk:** Partners not ready
- **Mitigation:** Flexible timeline, multiple touchpoints, incentives

### Paid Search Performance
- **Risk:** Low conversion rates
- **Mitigation:** Start with low budgets, test extensively, optimize quickly
