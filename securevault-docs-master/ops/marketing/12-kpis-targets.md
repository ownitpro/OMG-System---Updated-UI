# KPIs & Targets (First 90 Days)

## Primary Metrics

### Free Trial Signups

**Target:** 3–5% of unique visitors

**Measurement:**
- Unique visitors to homepage
- Signup form submissions
- Completed signups

**Benchmarks:**
- Week 1-2: 2-3% (foundation phase)
- Week 3-6: 3-4% (proof phase)
- Week 7-12: 4-5% (scale phase)

### Demo Requests

**Target:** 1–2% of unique visitors

**Measurement:**
- Demo page visits
- Demo form submissions
- Demo completions

**Benchmarks:**
- Week 1-2: 0.5-1% (foundation phase)
- Week 3-6: 1-1.5% (proof phase)
- Week 7-12: 1.5-2% (scale phase)

### Trial → Paid Conversion (Business)

**Target:** 20–30%

**Measurement:**
- Business trial signups
- Paid plan upgrades
- Conversion rate

**Benchmarks:**
- Week 1-4: 15-20% (early adopters)
- Week 5-8: 20-25% (optimization)
- Week 9-12: 25-30% (mature)

### Time-to-Aha (First Portal + Upload)

**Target:** <10 minutes

**Measurement:**
- Time from signup to first portal creation
- Time from signup to first upload
- Time to first share link

**Benchmarks:**
- Week 1-4: <15 minutes (baseline)
- Week 5-8: <12 minutes (improvements)
- Week 9-12: <10 minutes (target)

## Conversion Funnel

### Funnel Stages

1. **Visitor** → Homepage view
2. **Interested** → Features/Demo view
3. **Qualified** → Pricing view or Demo request
4. **Trial** → Signup
5. **Activated** → First portal/upload
6. **Converted** → Paid plan

### Funnel Targets

- Visitor → Interested: 30-40%
- Interested → Qualified: 10-15%
- Qualified → Trial: 20-30%
- Trial → Activated: 50-60%
- Activated → Converted: 20-30%

## Revenue Metrics

### Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)

**Target:** $5,000+ MRR by Week 12

**Measurement:**
- Active paid subscriptions
- Average revenue per user
- Monthly recurring revenue

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Target:** <$200 CAC

**Measurement:**
- Marketing spend
- Sales spend
- New customers acquired
- CAC = (Marketing + Sales) / New Customers

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Target:** $1,000+ LTV

**Measurement:**
- Average revenue per user
- Average customer lifetime
- LTV = ARPU × Average Lifetime

### LTV:CAC Ratio

**Target:** 5:1 or better

**Measurement:**
- LTV / CAC
- Higher is better
- Industry benchmark: 3:1
