## Interview Talking Points

### Opening Statement
"We built SecureVault Docs because we kept losing time chasing files across email and random drives. We wanted to make document workflows simple, secure, and predictable."

### Key Points
1. **Simplicity:** One link, no confusion
2. **Security:** PINs, watermarks, expiry dates
3. **Transparency:** Usage meters, clear pricing
4. **Client Experience:** Portals that clients actually use

### Common Questions

**Q: What makes SecureVault Docs different?**
A: We focus on simplicity and transparency. Client portals that clients actually use, secure sharing with clear controls, and usage meters so you always know where you are.

**Q: Who is your target customer?**
A: Small-mid firms managing recurring client paperwork - accountants, bookkeepers, real estate brokers, contractors. Also busy professionals organizing personal documents.

**Q: How does pricing work?**
A: Business plans are per-seat pricing, starting at $59.99/month. Personal plans are per-plan pricing, starting at $9.99/month. All with transparent usage meters and alerts.

**Q: What's next for SecureVault Docs?**
A: We're focused on expanding integrations, adding more templates to the marketplace, and building out enterprise features like SSO.
