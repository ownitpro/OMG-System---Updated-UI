# FAQs (Public-Facing)

## General

### What is SecureVault Docs?

A simple way to collect, organize, and share documents with clients or family, while staying in control of costs.

### How does it work?

Upload documents, organize with labels, share securely with PIN-protected links, and request documents from clients through secure portals. All with transparent usage meters and alerts.

### Who is it for?

**Business:** Small-mid firms (5-100 seats) managing recurring client paperwork - accountants, bookkeepers, real estate brokers, contractors, legal ops.

**Personal:** Busy professionals and families consolidating documents, organizing receipts, and preparing tax documents.

## Security

### Is my data secure?

Yes. You control access with PINs and expiry links. We log activity and support watermark viewing. All connections use HTTPS encryption, and data is encrypted at rest.

### Where is my data stored?

Data is stored securely in the cloud with industry-standard encryption. We follow security best practices and are working toward SOC 2 certification.

## Usage & Limits

### How do usage meters work?

We track key actions (storage, OCR pages, share links) and alert you at 70/80/90/95%. You can top up or upgrade before hitting the cap.

### What happens if I hit my limit?

You'll see alerts as you approach your limits. At 100%, new uploads are blocked. You can upgrade your plan anytime to increase limits.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime. Changes take effect immediately. Annual plans can be upgraded mid-cycle with prorated billing.

## Features

### Can I invite clients?

Yes. Send a single link to a client. They can upload files without creating an account. Each client gets their own secure portal.

### How do client portals work?

Create a portal for each client, send them a secure link, and they can upload documents directly. Everything lands organized in your vault.

### What is OCR?

Optical Character Recognition - automatic text extraction from documents. Makes your documents searchable by content, not just filename.

### Can I share documents securely?

Yes. Create PIN-protected, time-boxed share links with watermarks. Track access and revoke anytime.

A unique email address that automatically adds forwarded documents to your vault. Forward receipts, bills, or any documents, and they land organized with labels.
